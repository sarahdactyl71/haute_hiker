Hello, and Welcome to Haute Hiker!

Like any curious, and perhaps paranoid human being, you are worried about what the cusine will be like when society as we know it crumbles. Survival will depend on your ability to gather resources. Chances are, you will be eating your fair share of dehydrated camping food. Maybe you want to find the meal you will hate eating the least? Maybe things like, mouthfeel, honest branding, and spice variety will be hard to consider when you are looting a long abandonded outdoor store. It is my goal to make these choices painless, so that you can enjoy your meals as your last tether to the time that was. Welcome, you have come to the right place.

Okay, in all honesty you are probably a lot like me. You enjoy the outdoors. Nature is where you find yourself the most content. You love taking time away from the city to go camping or backpacking. You like to eat. You might even consider yourself a bit of a foodie. Chances are you have eaten some camping food when you thought "Good thing I am starving cause this is basically hot ham water". I made this blog for me, because I am curious to see how much food I can try, and if paying the few extra dollars will really boost my experience. I want to see if the dehydrated salmon alfredo is delicious, or if I will be in waves of regret as I shovel each bite into my hungry mouth.

I also made this blog for you. I know that there have to be more people out there who are curious about the austronaut ice-cream, or the packaged creme brulee dessert, but have never taken the plunge. You want to see what the texture of the huevos rancheros is like. You wonder what hacks can be made, if additions of hot sauce or a fried egg could maximize your outdoor culinary experience. I want to share my thoughts and experiments with you. Together we can not only broaden our horizons with exploration of the natural world, together we can enjoy food in those places. Thank you for taking the time to read this, and please, feel free to reach out.

-Your Friend at Haute Hiker
